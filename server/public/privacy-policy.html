Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 1, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Attendance Mobile App ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and organization details when you create an account.
  • Attendance Data: Time-in, time-out, date, location (project site), trade/profession, and vehicle registration information.
  • Project Information: Project names, codes, and associated attendance records.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and app version for troubleshooting and improvement purposes.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our attendance tracking service
  • To manage user accounts and authentication
  • To generate attendance reports and analytics
  • To send password reset emails and important notifications
  • To improve our app's functionality and user experience
  • To ensure security and prevent fraudulent activities

4. Data Storage and Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • All data is stored on secure servers with encryption
  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt encryption
  • We use HTTPS/SSL for all data transmission
  • Access to data is restricted to authorized personnel only
  • Regular security audits and updates are performed

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With project administrators within your organization for attendance management
  • When required by law or to comply with legal processes
  • To protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your attendance records
  • Opt-out of non-essential communications

8. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

10. Third-Party Services

Our app may use third-party services for:

  • Email delivery (for password resets and notifications)
  • Cloud hosting services

These third parties have their own privacy policies and we encourage you to review them.
